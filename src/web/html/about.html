We are building low-cost high-value ad-on boards for all your classic pinball machines. Vector (this device) currently supports all system 11 games and will be expanding to system 9, 7, 6, WPC, and DataEast games. Our goal is to provide the best possible experience for the player and the operator. We are constantly adding new features and improving the existing ones. We are always open to suggestions and feedback.
