Warped Pinball

Modern smarts for classic pinball games


Warped Pinball is a hobby, with a business license.
We're enthusiasts like you. We love the new games coming out today. We just think classic games have a few more tricks to learn

Our Mission

Bring modern features to classic games at accessible prices with unaltered gameplay


Our Team

Everyone helps with Warped Pinball on the side, for the love of Pinball. These are our dedicated team members.

Please contact us with ideas and feedback! We want to hear the good and bad about how Warped Pinball has effected you. We strive to respond as quickly as possible but do keep in mind that this is a second job for all of us.

Our Plan

We currently sell Vector boards with support for Williams/Bally System 9,11 and WPC games and plan to launch support for EM and Data East games in late 2025-early 2026

We are actively developing additional products to add more exciting features to your games
