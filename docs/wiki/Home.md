# Warped Pinball Vector Wiki

Vector brings WiFi connectivity, diagnostics, and modern tooling to classic pinball systems. This wiki collects the official quick starts, full manuals, and ROM management notes for every supported platform so you can get from unboxing to online scoring in minutes.

## Start Here
* New install? Jump straight to the [System WPC Quick Start](wpc/WPC_quick_start) or [System 11 Quick Start](sys11/sys11_quick_start).
* Need live walkthroughs? Watch the [installation playlist](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLviUqcd3jBxrLbbLz9X2bv3VnlM3TLe6F&si=zwGBoLwztZszCIlE).
* Have a question or bug report? [Open an issue](https://github.com/warped-pinball/vector/issues).
